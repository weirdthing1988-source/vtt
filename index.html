SMB VTT Version 1

Lightweight PlayCanvas VTT prototype with a simple tactical board, one billboard token, drag-and-drop token movement, and layered terrain editing.

Controls

  • Left click & drag on the board to move the token
  • W A S D pans the camera
  • Mouse wheel raises and lowers the camera height
  • Middle mouse button rotates the camera around the focus point
